Tokushima Ramen

Thanks to Kagawa Prefecture, the small,
remote Shikoku region may be better known
for udon, but Tokushima Prefecture's own
ramen style can definitely hold its own!
Tokushima ramen typically uses soy sauce
for its broth base, so the soup is often dark
brown. Pork and/or chicken bones are also
mixed in to enhance the flavor.

Ingredients

4 packets Chinese style
uncooked noodles

300 grams Thinly sliced pork belly

1 bag Bean sprouts

1/2 bunch Chopped green onion

2 tbsp Weipa

2 tbsp Soy sauce

1 tbsp Sugar

2 tbsp Sake

2 tbsp Soy sauce
