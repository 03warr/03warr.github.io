About Us

Our Goal

We at King Ramen aim to bring the many different
recipes of some of Japan's many regions directly
to a more global audience. We hope to help create
a fun, loving, and diverse community of ramen lovers!

Our History

King Ramen was founded in 2010
by Tomo Takashi and Sinichi Kudo, longtime friends, when
we were searching for our favorite ramen shops in the
United States. However, we quickly realized that enjoying
the traditional taste and flavour of ramen wasn't possible-
unless we made it ourselves.
